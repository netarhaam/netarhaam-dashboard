About UNICEF

UNICEF (United Nations Children's Fund) works in over 190 countries and territories to save children's lives, defend their rights, and help them fulfill their potential.

Why Support UNICEF?

  • Proven Impact: 70+ years of helping children
  • Transparency: 90% of funds go directly to programs
  • Global Reach: Works in every corner of the world
  • Emergency Response: First on the ground in crises
